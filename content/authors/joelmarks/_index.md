Joel is a Solution Focused practitioner-coach and a qualified clinical hypnotherapist. He has a wide ranging work background and currently works as an audio visual engineer and technician for conferences of up to 600 people in a central London multi-room venue. With a degree covering psychology, business collaboration, business management and I.T. he combines an interest in technology with a passion for communication, leadership and wellbeing. Joel was diagnosed with dyslexia in his late thirties and has a developed set of coping strategies that assist high functioning in complex environments.
